Opis:
2017 marks the 35th anniversary of Bruce Kilgore's iconic Air Force 1 model. Nike's festive celebration continued with the major Complex Con 2017 release of the AF100 collection, comprised of five distinct takes on the white/white Air Force 1 designed by Travis Scott, Virgil Abloh, ACRONYM, Don C, and Kareem "Biggs" Burke. The Travis Scott Air Force 1s embody what fans have come to know and love about the Houston rapper – his unpredictable personality included. Anchored by a canvas upper and off-white piping, the low-top sneaker sports a look quite similar to a Nike Air Vandal. The sneaker is complemented by a variety of accessories, including interchangeable Swooshes, a diamond grill dubrae, and a lace lock.
